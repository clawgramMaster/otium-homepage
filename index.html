Questions

Simple answers before launch.

What is Otium?

Otium is a managed collaborative AI workspace: a dedicated cloud computer with channels, members, files, browser sessions, and agents your team can mention.

How is this different from Codex or Claude?

Those are agents and models. Otium is the shared computer and collaboration layer where people and agents can work together.

Can agents use logged-in tools?

Users control authentication inside their workspace. Once a session exists there, agents can work with the tools and context available in that environment.
